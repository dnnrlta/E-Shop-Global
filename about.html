About us

This is an e-commerce that mainly sells Indonesian products. We as developers want to sell and introduce the products of these small micro and medium enterprises to the whole world.

Our goal is that we want to advance these micro, small and medium enterprises so that they can hone the creativity of the Indonesian people as well as the economy in Indonesia can be evenly distributed.

We hope that you can support us by always sending suggestions and criticism so that we can be even better.

Terima kasih saya saya ucapkan kepada teman - teman pengusaha yang telah ikut berkontribusi dengan menjual produknya melalui website ini.
